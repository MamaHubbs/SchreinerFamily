Together in Germany (1.3)

Adam and Emilie are believed to have first met in 1926. They married on **May 18, 1929**, at the Protestant Church in Oppau. This marriage was complicated by their religious differences (Adam was Catholic, Emilie was Protestant).

They emigrated due to the difficulty of their "mixed" marriage, the political climate (rumors Pop was unpopular with the Nazi movement), and limited employment opportunities.
