Emilie Georgina Baumann was born on 23 Apr 1911 in Oppau, Pfalz, Germany. She was the only daughter of Friedrich Baumann and Maria Dosch Baumann. Emilie's father, Friedrich, was employed as a locomotive engineer, ironworker, and foreman with the BASF in Ludwigshafen. Fortunately, he had not yet reported to work when the 1921 explosion occurred.

Emilie's brother, Karl Baumann, was killed as a captain in the German army during WWI. Emilie's siblings included Fritz, August, Karl, and Wilhem (Willie). Emilie had a summer home in Beimbach, near Rot am See in Wurttemberg, Germany, where she spent many summers.
